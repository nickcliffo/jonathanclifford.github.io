Budget Buddy

Budget Buddy is a user-friendly program designed to help individuals organize and manage their monthly finances. Users begin by entering their total monthly income, then add their expenses to build a complete financial overview. Once submitted, the program provides a clear breakdown showing starting income, total expenses, and remaining funds, making it easy to understand spending habits and maintain a balanced budget.
