Hi, I’m Jonathan Clifford

I’m a 25-year-old technology professional based in Springfield, Missouri, with a strong passion for problem-solving and continuous learning. After graduating high school in 2018, I began pursuing higher education while building my career at Jack Henry, where I’ve worked since 2019. Starting in the call center gave me a solid foundation in customer support, communication, and real-world technical issues.

Driven by curiosity and a desire to grow, I transitioned into the IT field in 2022, where I now work more directly with systems, troubleshooting, and technical solutions. I enjoy breaking down complex problems, learning how systems work behind the scenes, and finding efficient ways to improve processes. I actively work to grow my knowledge in programming every day, as my long-term goal is to become a professional programmer. I’m motivated by challenges, continuous improvement, and opportunities to build solutions that make a real impact for both users and businesses.

Outside of work, I’m happily married and the proud owner of two dogs who keep life fun and energetic. I’m passionate about hands-on technology — from building FPV drones from scratch to experimenting with 3D printing, custom PC builds, and other creative projects that blend engineering and innovation.
